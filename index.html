About Bao Mao 40

Bao Mao 40 brings the vibrant flavors of Asian street food directly to you! We specialize in fluffy, steamed bao buns and a variety of other delicious and fresh Asian dishes. Our mission is to serve up happiness, one bite at a time, ensuring every meal is a delightful experience. We're proud to be a mobile culinary adventure, bringing gourmet food to the streets of Landes and beyond!

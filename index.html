About Bao Mao 40

Bao Mao 40 brings the vibrant flavors of Asian street food directly to you! We specialize in fluffy, steamed bao buns filled with a variety of delicious and fresh ingredients. Our mission is to serve up happiness, one bao at a time, ensuring every bite is a delightful experience. We're proud to be a mobile culinary adventure, bringing gourmet food to the streets of Landes and beyond!
